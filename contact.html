Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the Korean name conversions?
Our conversions are based on phonetic approximations and may not be 100% accurate. Korean transliteration can vary based on regional dialects and personal preferences. The results are intended for entertainment and educational purposes.
Can I use the converted names commercially?
The converted names themselves are phonetic transliterations and can be used freely. However, please check our Terms of Service for specific usage guidelines regarding our service and generated images.
Why isn't my name converting correctly?
Some names may not have direct Korean equivalents or may require manual adjustment. If you're experiencing issues, please contact our support team with the specific name and we'll help improve our conversion system.
Do you store the names I convert?
Names are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for complete information about data handling.
Can you add more languages or styles?
We're always working to improve our service! Send us your suggestions at feedback@name2korean.com and we'll consider them for future updates.
Is the service really free?
Yes! Name2Korean is completely free to use. We may display advertisements to support the service, but all core features remain free for everyone.
